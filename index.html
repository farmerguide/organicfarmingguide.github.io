Bio-Fertilizers and Bio-Pesticides

Biofertilizers and biopesticides are environmental friendly products and can be used in integrated nutrient management (INM) and integrated pest management (IPM) techniques. These relates to the microorganisms and their role in enhancing soil fertility and disease and pest control for sustainable agriculture.
